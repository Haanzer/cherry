    How to Buy?

    Create your Wallet
    Start by downloading a wallet that supports the SOL Blockchain, such as SOLkeeper.
    Purchase SOL
    Before purchasing $CHERRY, make sure your SOL wallet is funded. SOL can be purchased on cryptocurrency exchanges such as Binance,Bybit and many other exchanges where SOLcoin is present.
    Visit SSOL.fi
    Go to SSOL.fi, connect your wallet and enter the $CHERRY token address. Select $CHERRY from the list and follow the prompts to confirm and sign the transaction with your wallet.
    Exchange SOL for $CHERRY
    Convert SOL to $CHERRY with no extra effort or fees. Keep in mind that slippage settings may need to be adjusted during periods of market volatility.
